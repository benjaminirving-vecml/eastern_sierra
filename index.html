About the Eastern Sierra

The Eastern Sierra is a stunning region of California known for its dramatic mountain landscapes, clear alpine lakes, and abundant outdoor recreation opportunities. It stretches along the eastern side of the Sierra Nevada mountain range.

Nature & Wildlife

The area is home to diverse wildlife including mule deer, black bears, and numerous bird species. The Eastern Sierra features iconic natural landmarks such as Mono Lake, Mammoth Lakes, and the ancient bristlecone pine forests.

Popular Activities

  • Hiking and backpacking
  • Fishing and boating
  • Skiing and snowboarding in winter
  • Photography and stargazing
